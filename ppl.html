What is PPL???

Push day is mostly upperbody training your muscles through a push motion. Some examples of these exercises are bench press, dumbell press, overhad press, triceps, lateral raies, dips, and arnod press.

Pull day is mostly upperbody training your muscles through a pull motion. Some examples of these exercises are deadlift, barbell row, cable row, dumbell curls, hammer curls, shrugs, farmers walks, and lat pull down.

Legs is a lower body day to train the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Some examples of these exercises are squats, leg press, leg extension, hamstring curls, glute bridges, lunges, and hip thrusts.
